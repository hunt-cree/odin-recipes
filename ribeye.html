Grilled Ribeye Recipe

All about the 'berta beef

Deliciously seared 'berta beef ribeye, less is more

    Ingredients

  • 1 'berta beef ribeye, minimum 1 and 1/2 inches thick
  • Salt
  • Pepper

    Steps

  1. Heavily salt and pepper each side of your 'berta beef
  2. Grill at 400, flipping once a minute for those good grill marks
  3. Down the hatch
